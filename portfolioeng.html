beAu - Goldsmith

I made this website for beAu, a small independent goldsmith in secondary occupation. beAu's atelier is located in Deinze. You can always go there by appointment for repairs, design or just to go take a look at the collection.

beAu often organises open-atelierdays or art weekends, where it collaborates with other small independent artists.
