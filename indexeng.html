Welcome to my portfolio!


Welcome to the portfolio of Joppe Minjauw. This portfolio is a final assignment for I1 Talent, a course in the first year IT at Hogent.

The intention of this portfolio is to show off both your webdesign skills and your languageflexibility. That's why you can visit this website in English and Dutch.

We also have to post all of our (individual) assignments we had to make for this course throughout the semester on this portfolio, so the lecturers can easily correct them.

 

What's there to see?


The page "About myself" speaks for itself. I introduce myself and briefly present my hobbies.

On "I1Talent" you'll find the assignments that had to be made (individualy) throughout the year for this course.

At "Portfolio" I present some of my own projects I've been working on.

"Links" gives you a short list of links that I personally use a lot in my daily life or have used during projects.
