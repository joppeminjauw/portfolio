Who am I?

My name is Joppe Minjauw and I was born the 30th of March 2000 in Deinze. I have 1 brother and 2 sisters, who all three already have a job. My brother's work is also IT related. It's mostly because of him that my interest in computer science is this big.

I skipped the 3th year of primary education, that's why I'm already studying at Hogent. In high school I've followed 6 years of Latin and graduated in Latin-science, which is something totally different from IT.

Soccer

Ever since I was 5 years old I've been playing soccer, 13 years in total. I played one year at Poesele and now 12 consecutive years at Sparta Petegem, where I am midfielder (6 & 8) at the U21. Last two seasons we became champion, both at the autumn break and at summer. This year we narrowly lost the championship, but ended as youngest team of the competition at a solid second place.
I've just recovered from a 1.5 year during injury to my shins and got to finish the end of the season.

Theater

Besides soccer, I've also acted for 7 years with an amateur theater company, Encachado. Two years ago I finished my "theater career" in style with a leading role as Robin Hood.

I got my theater genes without a doubt from my father. It's a dream of mine to be playing alongside my father in a play after I finished my studies.

Gaming

I've played games for as long as I can remember. I've gone through a lot of games, but it really started with the shooters War Rock, Quake and strategic games like Age of Empires, which I could play for hours on end with my brother.

The most competitive games I've played are League of Legends, Counter Strike: Global Offensive and at the moment Fortnite, sometimes to great annoyance of my parents.
